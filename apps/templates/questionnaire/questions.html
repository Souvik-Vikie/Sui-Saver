Questionnaire

  • Early identification and treatment of patients at elevated risk for suicide is a key suicide prevention strategy
  • The questionnaire aims to examine a patient on the basis of their recent happenings, medical history and general behavioral characteristics to find out whether he/she has mental depression or suicidal tendencies
  • We assume that the person taking this questionnaire is a close associate of the individual concerned (i.e., a family member/a mentor/a friend/a relative/a caregiver)
  • Please answer the questions as honestly as possible.
  • Although some questions may be optional, we request you to attempt maximum of them for most accurate results.
